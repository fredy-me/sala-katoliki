# Content Rights

Content rights are release-blocking for Sala Katoliki.

## Rule

Do not publish copyrighted Bible translations, Mass readings, devotional books, or other protected text unless Kilimanjaro Technology has written permission or valid licensing.

## Required Metadata

Where available, content should include:

- `source`
- `version`
- `last_updated`
- permission or licensing notes in internal review records

## Traditional Prayers

Traditional Catholic prayers may be public domain in many cases, but each source still needs review before store submission.

## Store Readiness

Before release:

- Review all prayer sources.
- Remove or replace unapproved copyrighted content.
- Ensure About includes content source and legal notes.
- Ensure support and privacy policy links are functional.
